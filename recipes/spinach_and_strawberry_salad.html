Spinach and Strawberry Salad

A Spinach and Strawberry Salad with a Dressing

We love this spinach strawberry salad all year round if we can find strawberries! Even the grandchildren love this quick and easy salad.

This strawberry salad probably doesn't taste any good but the guide want's me to add multiple recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika

How to make: step-by-step

  1. Step 1: Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl.
  2. Step 2: Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl; pour over spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
  3. Step 3: Enjoy!
